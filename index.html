Official Profile

A Legacy of Resilience & Administrative Excellence

A chemist by education and an administrator by heart, Oshin Sharma represents the new generation of civil servants in Himachal Pradesh. Holding an M.Sc in Chemistry (Honours) from Panjab University, her journey is marked by profound academic success and unwavering grit.

Despite missing the UPSC by a narrow margin of 5 marks, Oshin transformed setback into stepping stones. After clearing the BDO examination, she achieved Rank 10 in the Himachal Administrative Services, solidifying her place as a top-tier leader in the state governance.
